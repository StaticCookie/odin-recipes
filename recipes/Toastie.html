Ingredients

  • 2x Slices of bread
  • 1x Slice of cheese
  • Butter
  • Mayonese

Steps

  1. Butter one side of the bread and spread mayonese on the other side
  2. sandwhich cheese between the butter side of the 2 slices of bread
  3. Place the sandwhich on the hot pan making sure the mayonese side is touching the pan
  4. flip the sandwhich after 3-4 minutes of cooking (Make sure the cooked side is cooked to your liking)
  5. Remove sandwhich from grill after another 3-4 minutes
