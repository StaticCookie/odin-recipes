Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • 100g of mince meat
  • 2x Slices of Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Burger Sauce
  • Red Onion
  • Tomato
  • 2g Paprika
  • 2g Salt
  • 2g Pepper
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Mic the mince meat with the seasoning (Paprika, Salt, Pepper) & Bread crumbs
  2. Form the mince meat into palm sized meatballs
  3. Bring a Cast Iron pan to tempreature, once hot place meatballs into the pan and squish using a heavy flat utensil
  4. Once the cooked side begins to brown, use a spatula to flip the patty and flatten further, once completely flat place a slice of cheese on the cooked side and wait for the rest of the patty to cook
  5. Assemble the burger with sauce first, sauce is used as a glue to stick onion and lettuce to the top half of the bun
